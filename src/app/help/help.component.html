Game Instructions

MATCHING GAME

In this game, you will see a list of words in English and a list of words in Hebrew. Your goal is to draw a line between the English word and its corresponding Hebrew word. For example, if the word "Dog" appears in the English list and the word "כלב" appears in the Hebrew list, you need to connect them. This game helps you learn new words in both languages and practice matching words across different languages.

MIXED LETTERS

In this game, you receive an English word and a scrambled Hebrew word. Your goal is to figure out the correct Hebrew word. You need to try and rearrange the letters to form a meaningful and correct word. This game helps you practice word recognition, memory, and improve your ability to understand words in a language that is not your native tongue.

WORD SORTER

In this game, you are given a word and you need to decide if it belongs to a certain category. For example, if the given word is "Apple" and the category is "Fruits," you need to mark that it belongs to the category. This game develops your ability to sort and distinguish between different items based on the categories they belong to.

TRIVIA

In this game, you will be given a question with four possible answers. You need to choose the correct answer out of the four. This game is a great way to test your knowledge on various topics and improve your ability to make quick decisions based on limited information.
